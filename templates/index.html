Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EduLink

Is EduLink completely free?

Yes! EduLink is completely free to use with all core features including Pomodoro timer, task management, notes, and analytics. No credit card required.

Can I use EduLink on mobile devices?

Absolutely! EduLink is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Access your study dashboard anywhere, anytime.

How does the AI note-taking work?

Our AI analyzes your raw notes and converts them into well-structured, organized formats with proper headings, bullet points, and summaries - perfect for quick revision.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your personal data and study notes are completely private and never shared with third parties.

Can I collaborate with my study group?

Yes! EduLink offers study rooms, video classrooms, and shared notes features so you can learn together with your peers in real-time.
